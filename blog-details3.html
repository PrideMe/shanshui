What are the differences between various paper towels?

Time : 2023/06/01

In life, common household paper includes facial tissue, toilet paper, wet wipes, kitchen paper, etc. Each of them has different uses and cannot replace each other. Toilet paper is moderately soft and is mainly used when going to the toilet. Qualified toilet paper will not cause harm to the human body. Although the hygiene standards are not as high as those of facial tissue, it is abundant and cheap.

Wet wipes are special sterilization paper. Toilet paper manufacturers introduce that wet wipes contain certain sterilization ingredients. When it is inconvenient to wash hands when going out, wet wipes can effectively clean and sterilize. Wet wipes should not be used to wipe the face as much as possible. The facial skin is relatively fragile, and the sterilization ingredients in them can easily cause itching and redness.

Kitchen paper is special cleaning paper. Kitchen paper has good water and oil absorption. The paper is larger and thicker, which is very suitable for cleaning kitchen utensils, glass windows, mirrors, tables, etc. It is a veritable cleaning expert. In addition to household cleaning, kitchen paper can also clean food, absorb moisture and grease on the surface of ingredients before cooking, and make the ingredients fresher and cleaner.

Facial tissue paper is in direct contact with the face, and has very strict hygiene requirements. The state stipulates that the bacterial content of each gram of facial tissue paper shall not exceed 200 units. The requirements for toilet paper are relatively loose, and the bacterial content of each gram of toilet paper is not more than 600 units. In other words, the bacterial content of toilet paper is three times that of facial tissue paper. Using toilet paper to wipe your face may make it dirtier and dirtier.

Recycled materials are allowed in pure wood pulp. Toilet paper made of recycled materials meets national health standards. Waste paper other than household paper, medical paper and packaging paper can be processed into toilet paper. Facial tissue paper and napkin paper are specially used to clean the face. They are soft and skin-friendly, with high hygiene requirements. They can be used to wipe the mouth and face with confidence. In addition, facial tissue paper has better toughness after being soaked and is not easy to break. When wiping sweat, paper scraps will not remain on the face.

Fluorescent whitening agents are familiar to everyone. In order to make clothes cleaner, laundry detergent and washing powder often contain this substance. The state allows it to be added to daily necessities that do not come into direct contact with the human body. Facial tissues are in contact with the human face, and according to regulations, they must not contain migrating fluorescent whitening agents. Qualified facial tissues can be used with confidence and will not damage the skin.

However, the hygiene standards do not clearly require whether toilet paper contains migrating fluorescent whitening agents or how much they contain. Friends who are used to wiping their faces with toilet paper should pay attention.
